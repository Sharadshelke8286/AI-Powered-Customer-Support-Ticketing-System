# Step 5: Salesforce Developer Account Setup

## Objective

The purpose of this step is to create a Salesforce Developer Account which will be used to build and configure the AI-Powered Customer Support Ticketing System.

Salesforce Developer Org provides a free cloud-based environment for developers to build and test applications using Salesforce features.

---

# What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps businesses manage customer interactions, sales processes, customer support, and marketing activities.

It provides tools to:

* Manage customer relationships
* Track customer issues
* Automate business processes
* Analyze business data
* Provide customer service solutions

Salesforce stores data securely in the cloud and allows users to access the system from anywhere.

---

# Activity 1: Creating a Salesforce Developer Account

Follow the steps below to create a Salesforce Developer Org.

### Step 1

Open the Salesforce Developer signup page:

https://developer.salesforce.com/signup

### Step 2

Fill in the required information in the signup form.

Required details include:

* First Name
* Last Name
* Email Address
* Job Title: Developer
* Company Name: Your College Name
* Country: India

### Step 3

After entering the required information, click on **Sign Me Up**.

Salesforce will send a verification email to the registered email address.

---

# Activity 2: Account Activation

### Step 1

Open the email inbox used during signup.

### Step 2

Find the email sent by Salesforce and click **Verify Account**.

Note: The verification email may take **5–10 minutes** to arrive.

### Step 3

Set your account password and answer the security question.

### Step 4

Click **Change Password** to complete the setup.

### Step 5

After completing the process, you will be redirected to the **Salesforce Developer Org Setup Page**.

---

# Outcome

After completing this step, a Salesforce Developer Org is successfully created and ready for implementing the AI-Powered Customer Support Ticketing System.
