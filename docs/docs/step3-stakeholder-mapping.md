# Step 3: Stakeholder Mapping

## Objective

Stakeholder mapping identifies the key individuals and groups involved in the development, implementation, and usage of the AI-Powered Customer Support Ticketing System.

It helps define the responsibilities, interests, and interactions of each stakeholder to ensure the successful implementation and operation of the system.

---

## Overview

The Salesforce-based Customer Support Ticketing System involves multiple stakeholders who contribute to its development and usage. Each stakeholder group plays an important role in ensuring that customer issues are handled efficiently and that the system aligns with business objectives.

Effective stakeholder mapping ensures:

* Clear communication between teams
* Proper system access and responsibilities
* Alignment between business needs and technical implementation
* Smooth project execution

---

## Stakeholder Groups

### Customers

Customers are the end users who submit support requests when they face issues with products or services.

**Responsibilities**

* Submit support tickets
* Provide issue details
* Receive updates about case resolution

**Interest in System**

* Fast issue resolution
* Clear communication from support teams

---

### Support Agents

Support agents are responsible for handling customer issues and resolving support cases.

**Responsibilities**

* Review and manage support tickets
* Update case status
* Provide solutions to customers
* Communicate with customers during issue resolution

**Interest in System**

* Easy ticket management
* Automated assistance and suggestions
* Efficient case handling

---

### Support Managers

Support managers monitor support team performance and ensure SLA compliance.

**Responsibilities**

* Track case resolution performance
* Monitor SLA compliance
* Analyze support reports
* Improve support operations

**Interest in System**

* Visibility into support metrics
* Performance monitoring tools
* Operational insights

---

### System Administrators

System administrators manage the Salesforce platform and configure the ticketing system.

**Responsibilities**

* Configure objects, fields, and automation
* Manage user roles and permissions
* Maintain system security
* Ensure system performance and reliability

**Interest in System**

* Stable and secure platform
* Efficient system configuration
* Easy maintenance and scalability

---

### Business Stakeholders

Business stakeholders define the project requirements and ensure that the system supports business objectives.

**Responsibilities**

* Provide project requirements
* Validate system functionality
* Ensure alignment with business goals

**Interest in System**

* Improved customer service
* Operational efficiency
* Better customer satisfaction

---

## Conclusion

Stakeholder mapping ensures that all participants involved in the project clearly understand their roles and responsibilities. It helps maintain effective communication and ensures that the system is developed in alignment with both technical and business objectives.
